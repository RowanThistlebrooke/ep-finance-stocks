# 📈 Finance — Stocks tab

Adds a **Stocks tab** to your Vitality Finance tile, right beside your subscriptions:

- **Market brief** — today's market news tagged **buy / sell / watch**, with the best spots highlighted
- **Live holdings** — your stocks, priced through Finnhub, portfolio total, add-a-stock

Your subscription radar is **untouched** — this drops in *beside* it as a second tab. Purely additive.

---

## Connect it to your dashboard — one line

Open **Claude Code** inside your dashboard folder and paste:

> **Install the Stocks tab from `github.com/rowanthistlebrooke/ep-finance-stocks` into my Finance module.**

Claude reads [`INSTALL.md`](./INSTALL.md), wires it into **your** board — your setup, your data — and you're done. **No git, no merging, no conflicts.** That's the whole point: the app doesn't merge code, Claude installs it.

Then open your Finance tile → you'll see two tabs at the bottom: **subscriptions** and **stocks**.
